# CiteLocal AI Company Brief

## Decision

Build **CiteLocal AI**, a zero-budget service/software hybrid that sells AI answer visibility audits to local service businesses.

## Customer

Owner-operated local service businesses that depend on high-intent inbound demand: HVAC, plumbing, roofing, electrical, landscaping, pest control, dental, med spa, chiropractic, physical therapy, auto repair, detailing, pet grooming, tutoring, and childcare.

## Pain

Local operators already struggle to keep Google Business Profile, reviews, website pages, and SEO content current. AI search adds a new layer: being ranked is not the same as being recommended or cited. Most local websites do not state facts in a way that answer engines can quote confidently.

## Wedge

Start as a productized service using the audit generator plus manual review. This avoids paid APIs, avoids long SaaS build cycles, and can produce revenue with a simple static landing page plus direct outreach.

## Offer ladder

1. Free snapshot: lead magnet, top score + top 3 fixes
2. $149 full audit: report + 30-day content plan
3. $399 implementation sprint: schema, FAQ, and one service-area page
4. $49/mo monitoring: monthly re-scan + content brief

## Why this can be started near-zero budget

- Static site can be hosted free on GitHub Pages/Cloudflare Pages
- Audit CLI uses Python standard library
- Outreach can be manual via public websites and email forms
- Reports can be generated locally as HTML/PDF without paid data
- First customers can be fulfilled manually before automating

## 7-day validation target

- Build 25 free snapshots for local service businesses in one vertical
- Send concise personalized outreach to owners/agencies
- Goal: 5 replies, 2 sales calls, 1 paid audit or implementation sprint

## Risks

- AEO is noisy and vendor-hyped; avoid algorithm guarantee claims
- Some owners may not understand AI search yet; lead with missed high-intent customers, not jargon
- Website-only audit is incomplete without Google Business Profile/review data; manual add-ons can cover this during service delivery

## Success metric

First dollar collected from a local business for an audit or implementation sprint, without ad spend or paid software.
