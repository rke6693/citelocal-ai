# CiteLocal AI Sales/Growth Launch Package

This folder contains the zero-cost launch assets for selling CiteLocal AI as an immediate productized service: AI answer visibility audits for local service businesses.

## Recommended launch wedge

Start with **owner-operated HVAC companies in one metro**.

Why:

- High-value services.
- Clear local intent.
- Common website gaps.
- Owners understand missed calls.
- Manual audits can show specific value without paid tools.

## Package files

- `icp_and_offer_ladder.md` — niche ICP, buyer personas, positioning, pricing, and risk reversal.
- `lead_sourcing_zero_budget.md` — manual first-50 lead sourcing workflow using only free public sources.
- `cold_email_dm_scripts.md` — email, contact form, LinkedIn, Facebook/Instagram, follow-ups, and objection replies.
- `discovery_call_and_onboarding.md` — 20-minute sales call script, objections, and paid client onboarding checklist.
- `first_week_execution_plan.md` — day-by-day execution plan for the first seven days.
- `company_brief.md` — existing business brief and validation target.
- `outreach.md` — existing lightweight outreach reference.
- `implementation_plan.md` — existing implementation roadmap.

## Week-one targets

- Source 50 qualified prospects.
- Prepare 10 free AI visibility snapshots.
- Send 30 personalized manual messages.
- Generate 5 positive replies or snapshot requests.
- Book 2 discovery calls.
- Sell 1 paid audit or fix sprint.

## Launch offer ladder

- **Free:** AI Visibility Snapshot with top 3 fixes.
- **$149:** Full AI Answer Visibility Audit + 30-day content plan.
- **$399:** AI Visibility Fix Sprint with audit, FAQ/copy blocks, service-area improvements, and schema draft.
- **$49/month:** Monthly re-scan and content/update brief.

## Key operating rules

- Manual outreach only.
- Personalize every message with a concrete observed gap.
- Do not use paid tools or APIs.
- Do not promise rankings, AI citations, leads, or revenue.
- Do not build more software until there is a paid customer or strong demand signal.

## Fastest path to first sale

1. Pick one vertical and metro.
2. Build the first 50-lead list manually.
3. Identify the top 10 prospects with obvious website clarity gaps.
4. Send short, permission-based outreach.
5. Deliver snapshot summaries inline when requested.
6. Convert warm replies to a $149 audit or $399 fix sprint.
7. Use delivery feedback to refine the offer before automating.
