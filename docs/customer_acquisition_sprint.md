# CiteLocal AI — 7-Day Customer Acquisition Sprint

## Goal
Book the first 3 paid full audits without ad spend by giving highly specific free snapshots to local service businesses.

## ICP priority
1. HVAC, plumbing, roofing, pest control, restoration, landscaping, electricians.
2. Cosmetic dentistry, med spas, legal, urgent care, specialty clinics.
3. Multi-location local businesses with thin service-area pages.

## Daily target
- Find 20 prospects.
- Run 5 quick snapshots.
- Send 10 personalized emails/DMs.
- Follow up with every opened/replied lead in 48 hours.

## Qualification filters
- Public website exists.
- Local service area is visible but not strongly structured.
- Missing direct buyer FAQ or suburb/service pages.
- Reviews/proof exist somewhere but are underused on the site.

## Conversion path
Free snapshot -> show 3 concrete gaps -> ask if they want the full $149 audit -> offer $499+ implementation only after audit pain is clear.

## No-spend sourcing
- Google Maps manual search.
- Local chamber/member directories.
- Facebook community sponsor posts.
- Existing contractor/service directories.
- LinkedIn local owner search.

## First-message standard
Personalize one observation. Do not sound like mass SEO spam. Never promise rankings. Offer the free top-three fix list.
