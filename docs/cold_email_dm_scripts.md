# CiteLocal AI Cold Email and DM Scripts

## Messaging rules

- Keep the first touch short.
- Lead with a specific observed gap, not generic “AI SEO.”
- Do not imply guaranteed rankings, citations, traffic, or leads.
- Do not attach files in the first email unless requested.
- Ask for a small next step: “Want me to send it?” or “Worth a quick look?”
- Manually personalize every message.

## Core value proposition

> “I help local service businesses make their websites easier for AI answer engines and Google-style AI summaries to understand, trust, and quote.”

Plain-English version:

> “Your site should clearly answer the questions customers ask before they call: services, cities served, cost factors, emergency availability, reviews, license/insurance, and why they should trust you.”

## Personalization snippets

Use one per message:

- “Your site lists {{service}}, but I did not see a clear answer to {{buyer_question}}.”
- “The service area is mentioned in the footer, but not clearly on the main {{service}} page.”
- “I found reviews on Google, but the website does not surface much trust proof.”
- “I did not see FAQ or cost-factor content for {{service}}, which is often what AI summaries quote.”
- “The page title is generic and may not reinforce {{service}} in {{city}}.”
- “I did not see LocalBusiness/HVACBusiness schema in the page source.”
