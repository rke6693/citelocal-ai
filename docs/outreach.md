## Call script

- “How do most new customers find you today?”
- “Do you know which service pages actually bring calls?”
- “Have customers mentioned finding answers through ChatGPT, Google AI summaries, or voice search?”
- “If I could improve the clarity of your top service pages without a redesign, what service would you want more calls for?”

## Manual prospect filters

Good prospects:

- Site has no FAQ/cost/service-area pages
- Site has weak title/meta or no LocalBusiness schema
- High-ticket services, emergency demand, or repeat maintenance
- Owner-operated, not a large chain with existing agency

Avoid initially:

- National chains
- Sites behind heavy JS where simple reports fail
- Businesses with no clear contact path
- Highly regulated medical claims unless content stays conservative
